Missed/Cancelled Appointment Policy

Package Purchases

For no-show appointments or last-minute cancellations that do not conform to the policy below will result in that session being forfeited and will count towards the total sessions for that package. For treat-to-complete packages such as laser hair removal, the treat-to-complete offer will be null and void and the maximum number of sessions allowed for the purchased package will apply. Additional sessions will need to be purchased separately. Missed sessions will count toward the total allowed sessions for that package as listed below:

  • Laser Hair Removal: 6 sessions
  • Microneedling: 3 sessions
  • Tattoo Removal: 4 sessions
