Aminodine

Rehydrating spray

for mature skin

Description

DMK's "golden water" Aminodine is an age management formulation that contains natural extracts that could help revive mature, middle-aged, and sun-damaged skins. A refreshing hydrating spray that soaks into the skin helping to reactivate glucose and normalizing the free radical effect of glycation (reduces the ability of collagen fiber to regenerate leading to wrinkling, creping, and sagging of the skin).

Size

120 mL / 4 Fl. Oz. U.S.
