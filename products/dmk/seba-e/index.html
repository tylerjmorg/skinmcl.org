Seba-e

Hydrating oil

Description

Described as velvet radiance in a bottle, Seba-e is a fractionated blend of herbal oils and vitamin E sealing in moisture, helping combat dryness, dry lipid skin, dehydration, and skin suffering from transepidermal water loss (TEWL). When used with Herb & Mineral Mist, Seba-e will reestablish the protective acid mantle and promote barrier repair by replicating the natural way skin secretes oil.
