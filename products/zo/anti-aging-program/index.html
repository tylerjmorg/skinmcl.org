Anti-Aging Program

Description

The ZO® Skin Health Anti-Aging Program is a comprehensive skincare regimen designed to target and reduce the visible signs of aging. This program includes five products that work together to restore and maintain your skin's firmness and youthful glow.

Exfoliating Cleanser (Travel Size: 60 mL): This cleanser gently exfoliates and cleanses the skin, leaving it feeling soft, smooth, and refreshed. It contains microbeads and alpha-hydroxy acids to remove dead skin cells and unclog pores.

Exfoliating Polish (Travel Size: 16.2 g): This polish exfoliates and removes dead skin cells, revealing smoother, brighter skin. It's also infused with vitamins and antioxidants to help protect the skin against environmental stressors.

Complexion Renewal Pads (Travel Size: 30 Pads): These pads help to reduce the appearance of fine lines, wrinkles, and other visible signs of aging. They are infused with alpha-hydroxy acids and antioxidants to improve the texture and tone of the skin.

Daily Power Defense (Travel Size: 30 mL): This serum is formulated with antioxidants and peptides to help protect the skin against environmental stressors and improve the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles.

Growth Factor Serum (Full Size: 30 mL): This firming serum is designed to promote the production of collagen and elastin that help to maintain the skin's elasticity.
