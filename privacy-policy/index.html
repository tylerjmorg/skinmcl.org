Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 8, 2025


At Skin Medical Cosmetics & Laser, your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit our website. Please note that you agree to this policy when you visit skinmcl.org and/or use any our services. All products and service are subject to our privacy policy.


Information We Collect

While using our website, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Name (if voluntarily submitted through forms)
  • Email address (if voluntarily submitted)
  • Phone number (if voluntarily submitted)
  • IP address (automatically collected)
  • Other information relevant to our services

Please note: This information may not be collected in every visit and is only gathered through specific interactions or website tools.


How We Collect Information

We may collect data through:

  • Cookies: Small files stored on your browser to improve your browsing experience.
  • Google Analytics: A third-party service used to analyze website traffic and usage trends.
  • Google Maps & Fonts: Embedded tools that may collect technical and usage data via Google services.
  • Instagram & Meta Embeding: Embedded tools that may collect technical and usage data via Meta services.
  • RepeatMD Embeding: Embedded tools that may collect technical and usage data via RepeatMD services.

Why We Collect Information

We collect this data to:

  • Ensure website functionality (e.g., embedded Google Maps)
  • Improve user experience and website performance
  • Monitor site usage to better serve visitors

Sharing of Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties. Data collected through services like Google Analytics is subject to Google's own privacy policies.


Data Security

We take appropriate measures to protect your information, but please note that no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.


Your Choices

You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings. However, this may affect your experience on our site.


Third-Party Services

Our website uses third-party services including:

  • Google Analytics
  • Google Fonts
  • Google Maps API
  • Meta (Facebook & Instagram) APIs
  • RepeatMD - MyRepeat API

These services may collect data according to their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review Google's, Meta's, & RepeatMD - MyRepeat's Privacy Policy for more details: https://policies.google.com/privacy, https://www.facebook.com/privacy/policy/, https://myrepeat.com/privacy-policy.html


Children's Privacy

Our website is not intended for children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from anyone under this age.


Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised "Effective Date."
