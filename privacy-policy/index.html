Information We Collect

While using our website, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Name (if voluntarily submitted through forms)
  • Email address (if voluntarily submitted)
  • Phone number (if voluntarily submitted)
  • GitHub automatically collects Internet Protocol (IP) addresses for security purposes.
  • Other information relevant to our services, for example, user agent information.

Please note: Most of this information may not be collected in every visit and is only gathered through specific interactions or website tools.

How We Collect Information

We may collect data through:

  • Cookies: Small files stored on your browser to improve your browsing experience.
  • Google Analytics: A third-party service used to analyze website traffic and usage trends.
  • Google Maps & Fonts: Embedded tools that may collect technical and usage data via Google services.
  • Instagram & Meta Embedding: Embedded tools that may collect technical and usage data via Meta services.
  • TikTok Embedding: Embedded tools that may collect technical and usage data via TikTok services.
  • Boulevard Embedding: Embedded tools that may collect technical and usage data via Boulevard services.

Why We Collect Information

We collect this data to:

  • Ensure website functionality, for example, embedded Google Maps or Instagram embeds.
  • Improve user experience and website performance.
  • Monitor site usage to better serve visitors.

Sharing of Information

We do not personally share your personal information with third parties. Data collected through third-party services, like Google Analytics, is subject to their own privacy policies.

We will not share mobile information or personally identifiable information with third parties for marketing or promotional purposes.

Data Security

We take appropriate measures to protect your information, but please note that no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. The use of our website is at your own risk.

Text Communication

We may communicate with you via SMS text message for purposes related to your bookings, such as appointment reminders and updates. Text messages from our third-party service providers are subject to their own privacy policies.

By entering your phone number, you are agreeing to receive booking text messages from Skin - Medical Cosmetics & Laser. Message & data rates may apply. Message frequency may vary. Reply HELP for more information. You can reply STOP or UNSUBSCRIBE to opt-out.

Your Choices

You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings. However, this may affect your experience on our site.

Content Blockers

The use of content and/or ad blockers is welcome on our website.

Please note that some content may be blocked when they are used, for example, social media and rewards-embedded content. Some fonts may be blocked as well.

JavaScript

The purpose of our JavaScript files is to bring you a rich and consistent experience across all of our web pages. No Personally Identifiable Information (PII) is intentionally collected through our code. Please note that all of our pages require JavaScript to function.

Children's Privacy

Our website is not intended for persons under the age of 18, and we do not knowingly collect information from anyone under this age.
