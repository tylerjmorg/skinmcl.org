Laser Tattoo Removal

Laser tattoo removal uses lasers to break down tattoo ink particles in the skin. The laser targets the pigment colors in the tattoo, causing them to fragment into smaller particles that are then absorbed by the body's immune system and eliminated naturally. Multiple sessions are usually required to completely remove a tattoo, and the number of sessions depends on various factors such as the size, location, and color of the tattoo. Laser tattoo removal is considered a safe and effective way to remove tattoos without scarring or damage to the surrounding skin.
