Cosette Bailey

Licensed Master Esthetician

Med Spa Manager

Cosette is a Certified Nursing Assistant (CNA) and a licensed Master Esthetician.

She has completed her nursing prerequisites at Snow College - Richfield and became a CNA through Bridgerland Technical College in 2015. With a decade of experience in healthcare, she has also pursued additional training in areas such as Medication Tech and Phlebotomy. In February 2024, she graduated from Paul Mitchell The School Logan with her Master Esthetics license, specializing in advanced facials, chemical peels, dermaplaning, laser therapy, and radio frequency microneedling.

In her spare time, she enjoys cuddling with her baby girl, watching reality TV, and exploring the outdoors with her mom and dogs!
