David Skalka Jr., NP

Medical Director

David Skalka Jr., NP is a board certified Family Nurse Practitioner (FNP) who is currently employed at the Salt Lake City VA Medical Center, Kaysville Family Medicine, and CGSN. He was recently awarded an internship as a Department of Veterans Affairs Geriatric Scholar. He has served as on the Board of Directors for the Utah Ambulatory Association (UAA) where he actively participated in lobbying for legislation at the Utah Capitol. He has started several successful small businesses.

His education started at West Penn Hospital School of Nursing (Pittsburgh, PA) with a diploma in nursing. He completed his BSN at the University of Phoenix (Salt Lake City, UT) and MSN at Frontier Nursing University (Lexington, KY) in family practice. He completed his Juris Doctor at Concord Law School (Los Angeles, CA) with a minor in healthcare policy. David is an avid golfer, eagle scout, veteran of the United States Army, and president of college class.
