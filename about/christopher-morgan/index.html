Medical Director

435-716-8765

Chris completed a dual specialization in Family and Emergency Medicine at the University of South Alabama in 2010, followed by additional specialty training in Emergency and Trauma Medicine through the same institution's residency program in 2013.

He has extensive experience in family medicine, emergency and trauma care, with additional focus in endocrine and psychiatric medicine. His clinical interests include bariatric and weight-loss management, thyroid disorders, and diabetes. Chris has held admitting privileges for inpatient psychiatric care at Davis Hospital in Layton, UT, and Jordan Valley Medical Center in West Valley, UT.
